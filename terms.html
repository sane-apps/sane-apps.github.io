1. What SaneApps Is

SaneApps is a collection of open source macOS utilities built by an independent developer. The apps include SaneBar, SaneClip, SaneHosts, SaneClick, and others as released. All source code is available on GitHub under the GPL v3 license.

2. How You Get the Software

You can obtain SaneApps software in two ways:

  • Build from source — Clone the repository and build it yourself. Free, always.
  • Buy the DMG — Purchase a signed, notarized build for $5 per app. This supports continued development.

Purchases are processed by Lemon Squeezy, who acts as the Merchant of Record. Their terms and privacy policy apply to transactions.

3. What You Get

When you purchase a DMG, you receive a one-time download of a signed, notarized macOS application. There is no subscription. Updates are provided via Sparkle (in-app updater) at no additional cost for the current major version.

4. Refunds

If the software doesn't work on your Mac, contact hi@saneapps.com and I'll make it right. I handle refunds on a case-by-case basis through Lemon Squeezy.

5. No Warranty

The software is provided "as is" without warranty of any kind. I do my best to keep it working on the latest macOS, but I can't guarantee compatibility with every hardware and software configuration.

6. Your Data

SaneApps collects zero data. No accounts, no analytics, no telemetry, no network calls. Everything stays on your Mac. See the Privacy Policy for details.
